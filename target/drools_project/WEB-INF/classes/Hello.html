Environmental Responsibility toolkit for SMEs

This toolkit will help small and medium-sized enterprises to improve their environmental impact. By mapping company's energy use with its operations you will create understanding and increase awareness, helping your organization to become greener. Simply fill the forms and get results along with recommendations.

Start Assessment

About project

Assessment is based on 5 categories:
* Equipment procurement compliance with Green ICT guidelines and Optimizing enterprise operations;
* Energy performance improvement and monitoring and Use of alternative energy;
* Energy aware networks engineering adherence;
* Social commitment;
* Waste Management;
With this toolkit SMEs can discover conveniently classified list of activities on how to save on energy costs and move towards carbon netural business.
