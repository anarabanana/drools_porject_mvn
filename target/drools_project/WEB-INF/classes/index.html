Environmental Responsibility Toolkit for SMEs

This toolkit has been designed to help small and medium-sized enterprises improve their environmental impact. By mapping a company's energy use with its operations and following behavioural best practices a company can increase its understanding and awareness in its environmental responsibility, helping the organization to become greener. Simply fill the forms and get results along with recommendations for your organization.

Start Assessment

About This Project

Companies nowadays seem to show an increasing commitment to a more sustainable behaviour. Environmental responsibility is a pillar of a broader corporate social responsibility term.
Assessment is based on 5 categories:
Equipment procurement compliant with Green ICT guidelines and the optimization of enterprise operations;
Energy performance improvements and monitoring towards the use of alternative energy resources;
Energy- aware network engineering adherence;
Social commitment;
Waste management;
With this toolkit SMEs can discover conveniently classified list of activities on how to save on energy costs and move towards a carbon neutral business.
The methodology behind the assessment is based on Belief-Rule-Based Reasoning. The Drools Inference Engine is deployed to this end.
