Environmental Responsibility toolkit for SMEs

This toolkit will help small and medium-sized enterprises to improve their environmental impact. By mapping company's energy use with its operations and following behavioral best practices you will create understanding and increase awareness, helping your organization to become greener. Simply fill the forms and get results along with recommendations.

About project

Companies nowadays seem to show an increasing commitment to a more sustainable behaviour. Environmental responsibility is a pillar of a broader Corporate Social Responsibility term.
Assessment is based on 5 categories:
Equipment procurement compliance with Green ICT guidelines and Optimizing enterprise operations;
Energy performance improvement and monitoring and Use of alternative energy;
Energy aware networks engineering adherence;
Social commitment;
Waste Management;
With this toolkit SMEs can discover conveniently classified list of activities on how to save on energy costs and move towards carbon netural business.
The methodology behind the assessment is based on Belief Rule-Based reasoning, Drools inference engine is deployed.
